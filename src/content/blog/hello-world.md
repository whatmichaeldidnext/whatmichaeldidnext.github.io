# Hello, World!

Welcome to the new **Insights** section.

This area complements the interactive portfolio by providing short technical notes, project reflections, and articles exploring the decisions behind the work shown elsewhere on the site.

Rather than documenting every detail of a project, these posts capture lessons learned, architectural decisions, interesting technical challenges, and ideas worth sharing.

Think of this as an engineering notebook that grows alongside the portfolio.
