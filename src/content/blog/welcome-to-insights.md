# Welcome to Insights

Welcome!

This portfolio has always been intended to be more than a list of jobs or a downloadable CV. Its purpose is to explore the relationships between projects, technologies, skills and ways of thinking that have shaped my professional journey.

The interactive graph shows *what* I've worked on.

The project pages explain *what* was delivered.

This **Insights** section explains *why*.

## Why write?

Every project leaves behind more than its deliverables. There are architectural decisions, unexpected challenges, technical discoveries and lessons that rarely fit neatly into a project summary.

Those are the things I want to capture here.

Sometimes an article will explore a specific technology. Other times it may explain why a particular design decision was taken, reflect on a delivery approach, or simply document something interesting I learned while solving a problem.

## What you'll find

Over time this section will include:

- Technical deep-dives into projects and architectures
- Reflections on programme delivery and leadership
- Notes on data, software engineering and infrastructure
- Experiments and prototypes
- Research and learning from ongoing study
- Thoughts on cybersecurity, networking and emerging technologies

Some posts will be short observations. Others may become detailed case studies that link back into the wider portfolio.

## An evolving portfolio

This site is deliberately treated as a living project.

It evolves alongside my experience, and the technology behind it changes as I continue learning. Many of the techniques used here—from interactive relationship graphs to content modelling and CMS integration—have themselves become projects worth documenting.

In that sense, the portfolio is both a showcase **and** one of the things being showcased.

Thanks for visiting, and I hope you find something useful.
